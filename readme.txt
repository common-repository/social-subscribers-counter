Social Subscribers Counter is a plugin which display your twitter followers, facebook fans, and feedburner subscribers with a widget and also in the "Right Now" panel of Admin.

== Description ==

This plugin allow you to display your Twitter followers, Facebook fans, and Feedburner readers in your website with a widget and also in the "Right Now" Panel of Admin Dashboard.

You just have to fill three fields. If you don't want to display a count, just let it blank. But, if your field is misconfigured, it will display an error.

Translation: English, French

== Installation ==

1. Upload the complete `social-subscribers-counter` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Go to 'Social Subscriber Counter' tab under the 'Settings' tab and configure the plugin.
